Site Plan – Reciclá Argentina

Site Name

Reciclá Argentina

This name was chosen because it clearly and directly communicates the purpose of the site: to educate, connect, and promote recycling throughout Argentina.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as an educational and informative resource for people in Argentina interested in recycling and sustainability. It will include practical guides, recycling locations, and environmental news.

Scenarios

  • Where can I recycle batteries or electronics in my neighborhood?
  • What type of waste is accepted at the nearest green collection point?

Color Schema

Forest Green (#5E6C5B): Headings and highlight elements

Fresh Cream (#F4EFE6): Section backgrounds

Cloud White (#FEFCF6): General page background

Sky Blue (#D6E0E2): Decorative elements

Storm Cloud (#686867): Secondary text, outlines

Midnight (#162A2C): Main body text

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for all site headings and titles.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text and general content.
